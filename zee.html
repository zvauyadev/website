About Ferreira's Dorp

Ferreira's Dorp is remembered as the birthplace of Johannesburg, where discovery, ambition and community came together to shape one of South Africa's most influential cities.

In 1886, Colonel Ignatius Ferreira established what became known as Ferreira's Camp after gold was discovered on the Witwatersrand. What began as a temporary settlement soon grew into a thriving community and ultimately the foundation of Johannesburg.

“Gathered by Purpose, Bound by Place”

Historical Timeline

  • 1886: Gold discovered on the Witwatersrand.
  • 1886: Ferreira's Camp established.
  • 1890: A growing community forms around the gold fields.
  • 1900+: Johannesburg emerges as a major city.

A Land Transformed by Discovery

Located on the eastern side of Johannesburg's CBD, the site still holds traces of mining activity, movement paths and early settlement patterns that shaped the city's development.

The landscape preserves memories of those who arrived seeking opportunity and who unknowingly helped create the foundations of a future metropolis.

The Golden Cradle

The Golden Cradle is more than a destination; it is a journey through time.

Rooted in the story of Ferreira's Dorp, the experience invites guests to connect with the land, the history and the spirit of community that transformed a mining camp into the cradle of Johannesburg.
